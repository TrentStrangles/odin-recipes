Pumpkin Juice

The flavor:

The flavor of pumpkin juice is really mostly apple cider and pumpkin spice with hints of pumpkin and apricot that give it a really full-bodied and smooth appeal.

Ingredients:

  • 300grams of pumpkin
  • 300ml water
  • 2tbsp honey
  • 2tbsp grandulated sugar(or more as desired)
  • milk(as needed)

Steps:

  1. Remove pumpkin seeds and skin. Cut into 1 cm thick slices. Put in a small pot, add water, and simmer over medium heat. When it becomes soft, remove from heat, and cool.
  2. Add step 1's ingredients to a mixer. Add honey, and sugar, mix. Then chill in refrigerator.
  3. Pour step 2's ingredients into a glass. Add milk as desired. Drink while mixing.
